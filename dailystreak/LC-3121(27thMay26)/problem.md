## Problem Statement

You are given a string `word`. A letterá`c` is called **special** if it appears **both** in lowercase and uppercase in `word`, and **every** lowercase occurrence of `c` appears before the **first** uppercase occurrence of `c`.

Return the number of* ***special** letters* *in* *`word`.

á

Example 1:

**Input:** word = "aaAbcBC"

**Output:** 3

**Explanation:**

The special characters are `'a'`, `'b'`, and `'c'`.

Example 2:

**Input:** word = "abc"

**Output:** 0

**Explanation:**

There are no special characters in `word`.

Example 3:

**Input:** word = "AbBCab"

**Output:** 0

**Explanation:**

There are no special characters in `word`.

á

**Constraints:**

	- `1 <= word.length <= 2 * 10^5`

	- `word` consists of only lowercase and uppercase English letters.

## Hints

1. For each character c, store the first occurrence of its uppercase and the last occurrence of its lowercase.
